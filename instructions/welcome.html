Welcome!

In the following task, you will be presented with pairs of options, and your task is to choose in each pair the option you prefer.

In each trial, you will choose between two amounts of money that can be granted at certain probabilities. For example, you might choose between winning $5 for sure, and some percent of winning a greater prize.

Your choices determine how much bonus you get! At the end of the task, one of the choices you made will be randomly chosen, and you will win the amount of money resulting from your choice on that trial. To begin the task, press the button below.

When you are ready to start, press the "Start" button.
